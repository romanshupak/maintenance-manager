Our Vessel Positions

Master

The Master is responsible for the overall command of the vessel and ensures the safety of the crew and cargo.

Chief Officer

The Chief Officer assists the Master and oversees the deck crew, ensuring the vessel's safe and efficient operation.

2nd Officer

The 2nd Officer is responsible for navigation and maintaining navigational equipment, ensuring the vessel stays on course.

3rd Officer

The 3rd Mate assists in navigation, safety drills, and maintenance tasks, supporting the higher-ranking officers.

Chief Engineer

The Chief Engineer oversees the engine room and all mechanical systems, ensuring the vessel's propulsion and power needs are met.
